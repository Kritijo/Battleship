How to Play Battleship

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia:

Battleship (also known as Battleships) is a strategy guessing game for two players. It is played on ruled grids (paper or board) where each player's fleet of warships is placed. The locations of the fleets are concealed from the opponent. Players take turns calling "shots" at the other player's ships, and the goal is to destroy the opponent's fleet.

Game Rules
